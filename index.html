About This Space

Welcome to "Efcharis," where the fresh aroma of pine and sea breeze greet you! Nestled in Neos Klima, Efcharis offers you moments of pure tranquility and relaxation, surrounded by nature and just a short walk from some of the island’s most beautiful beaches—Hovolo, Kastani, Ftelia, Milia, and the famous Ai Giannis (from "Mamma Mia"), among others. This lovingly crafted home will make you feel at ease from the very first moment.

The Space

This charming 76-square-meter house is ideal for unwinding with family or friends. Designed with meticulous attention to detail, it offers supreme comfort and relaxation. The spacious corner sofa in the open living area invites restful moments, while the large dining table is perfect for memorable meals and gatherings.

Two cozy bedrooms feature large beds, and a fully equipped bathroom includes hair dryers and a washing machine. The modern kitchen is ready for you to prepare your favorite dishes.

Enjoy two beautiful terraces: the west-facing terrace offers a dining table and chairs—perfect for peaceful meals or afternoon drinks. The east-facing terrace has sun loungers for soaking up the sun or relaxing with a book.

Neighborhood & Amenities

Efcharis embodies peace and comfort. Nearby you'll find pristine beaches, shops, pharmacies, and tavernas—all within easy walking distance. The bus stop is just 100 meters away, and stunning bays are about 3 km from the house, ready for unforgettable seaside adventures.
